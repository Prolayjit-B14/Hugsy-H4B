Emma's Brave Words

Emma loves stories, but reading in class makes her nervous. Today, her teacher asks her to read a short paragraph aloud.

She feels her heartbeat quicken as she looks at the words. Sometimes, the letters seem to dance on the page, making it hard for her to read.

Her best friend Max smiles at her from across the room, encouraging her. She knows she has two choices.
